EXPERIMENT 01

AIM

To prepare methane in the laboratory.

MATERIAL REQUIRED

sodium acetate, sodium hydroxyde, Burner, Delivery tube, Boiling tube

THEORY

Methane can be prepared in the laboratory bu heating sodium acetate with soda lime (in the ratio of 1:4) in the boiling tube. The methane produced is collected over water because its insoluble in water

PROCUDURE

  1. Take a mixture of sodium acetate and soda lime in a boiling tube.
  2. Set the appartus according to the diagram.
  3. On the heating a gas is evolved.
  4. Gas is collected by the downward displacement of water.

OBSERVATION

Put a burning stick into the jar. It burns in air with a bluish flame.
